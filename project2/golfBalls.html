The Diffrent Types of Golf Balls

For beginner golfers it is not that big of a deal to pick the correct right golf ball, however for pros and advanced golfers, picking the right golf ball is very critical. Golf balls have different number of layers and have compression ratings, some have more spin than others and some are much softer. Here are some of the different types of golf balls on the market.

  • Tour Performance
  • Tour value
  • Straight Distance
  • Soft Distance

Tour Performance

Tour performance have 3-5 layers, these balls are usually the most expensive on the market, they are designed for optimal distance, feel and spin. To get the most benefit from this ball you will need a faster swing speed.

Straight Distance

Straight distance has 2-3 layers, and these balls are built with a large core and thin outer layer which is optimal for maximum distance. However, these balls don’t have much short-game performance due to wedge spin.

Soft Distance

As you noticed each of the different balls on the market have a different number of layers. Golf balls have multiple layers because it allows players to get the optimal feel, distance, and backspin. Additional layers will help players with a faster swing speed get more spin with their wedges and less spin with their driver.

Ball Layers

number of layers Description
1 These balls are used on mini golf courses if used on the course they won’t go far or last long
2 These balls are perfect for beginners who need to get the max distance. They have a large core and a thin outer layer.
3 These balls have an addition layer that will add short game spin to the ball
4 These balls are made for people who swing the club faster to help them get more spin.
5 These balls are not common but make the softer than the 4-layer ball
