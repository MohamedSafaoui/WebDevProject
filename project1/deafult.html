A lITTLE ABOUT GOLF

Golf originated in the 15th century in Scotland but is a sport played around the world. Golf is a club & ball sport, but unlike other sports the player with the lower score wins. The fun part of golf is that there is no standard playing area and you can play in different spots and explore nature. Golf Courses usually have 9 or 18 holes, each hole starts with the teeing ground or also know as “tee box”, along with the teeing ground a course consist of a fairway, the rough and hazards like water and sand traps. In order, to maneuver around these hazards and make your ball into the hole there are many different clubs to use for your advantage depending on what position you are in. In this website we will explore diffrent aspects of golf such as the diffrent clubs, golf balls, and some beginner golf tips to help you on the course.
