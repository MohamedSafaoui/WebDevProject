Intro

A Little About Golf

Golf originated in the 15th century in Scotland but is a sport played around the world. Golf is a club & ball sport, but unlike other sports the player with the lower score wins. The fun part of golf is that there is no standard playing area and you can play in different spots and explore nature. Golf Courses usually have 9 or 18 holes, each hole starts with the teeing ground or also know as “tee box”, along with the teeing ground a course consist of a fairway, the rough and hazards like water and sand traps. In order, to maneuver around these hazards and make your ball into the hole there are many different clubs to use for your advantage depending on what position you are in. In this website we will explore diffrent aspects of golf such as the diffrent clubs, golf balls, and some beginner golf tips to help you on the course.

Golf Clubs

The Diffrent Types of Golf Clubs

Woods

The woods clubs consist of the driver and the fairway woods. Although they are called woods their clubheads are no longer made from wood. The reason to pick a club from the woods category would be because golfers are able to swing them the fastest and are used for long shots from the teeing ground. These are usually peoples favorite, not only becasue of the long range but, also how satisfying a perfect stike sounds.

Irons

Irons come in numbered sets ranging from 3-iron to 9-iron. Irons have smaller clubheads than woods and are usually very thin. Irons are generally used on shots from the fairway. The lower the iron number the further the ball should travel

Hybrids

Hybrids are a newer category of golf clubs. Just like the name itself, it is a hybrid between the woods and the irons. Just like the irons they are numbered, and the number corresponds to the iron they replace.

Wedges

The wedges category includes the pitching wedge, gap wedge, sand wedge and lob wedge. Wedges are their own type of golf club. Wedges are used for shorter shots into the greens, pitches around the green and are very helpful for playing out of sand traps.

Putters

Putters comes in the widest varieties of shapes and sizes. Putters are used for putting in the putting greens to make the ball into the hole.

Golf Balls

The Diffrent Types of Golf Balls

For beginner golfers it is not that big of a deal to pick the correct right golf ball, however for pros and advanced golfers, picking the right golf ball is very critical. Golf balls have different number of layers and have compression ratings, some have more spin than others and some are much softer. Here are some of the different types of golf balls on the market.

  • Tour Performance
  • Tour value
  • Straight Distance
  • Soft Distance

Tour Performance

Tour performance have 3-5 layers, these balls are usually the most expensive on the market, they are designed for optimal distance, feel and spin. To get the most benefit from this ball you will need a faster swing speed.

Straight Distance

As you noticed each of the different balls on the market have a different number of layers. Golf balls have multiple layers because it allows players to get the optimal feel, distance, and backspin. Additional layers will help players with a faster swing speed get more spin with their wedges and less spin with their driver.
