How it works.

Every month we collectively “photo club” members will have the choice to either submit their own category or vote on rotating categories. Once the category of the month has been selected it will be on the header of the category page under category of the month. The website is automatically updated on 12:00 am for every first of the month. After voting has been concluded, the winner/winners receive a permanent spot in the category page and recent winners will be showcased right next to the recent categories.
