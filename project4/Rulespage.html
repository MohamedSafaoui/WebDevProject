Rules

After you take your amazing photo and are ready to upload them, please make sure you follow the proper rules in order for your photo to qualify for voting. Any submissions that do not adhere to every rule will be automatically disqualified and removed from the site.


All pictures that are uploaded to photo club must use one of the following image formats: gif, png, or jpg. All other file formats will not be accepted so please make sure to double check your file type. Due to file size limitations file submissions greater than 1MB will be automatically removed. All submissions must follow the current catagories of the month. All images must be appropriate, no exceptions!

All photos must be your own original content in order to qualify for voting. All submissions must be submitted in before 11:59pm on the last day of the month. Voting will open 1 week after the submission due date. After voting ends winners will receive an email and will be uploaded to the categories page of the winner of that specified category. Once you have read the rules then make sure to head to the submit page to upload your photo, and have it ready to be voted on.
