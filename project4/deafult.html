Welcome to the photo club!!!

what is photo club?

Photo club is the best interactive photo club for Macomb community college students! What makes Photo the best? The number of interactive features that brings photo club together like the live chat room, interactive voting, galleries, and dozens of categories! Photo club is open to all Macomb students of all majors! If you are a photographer and want to showcase your work to other students Photo club is the best place to do that. Want to collaborate and communicate with other students who are also interested in photography? Make sure to utilize the live chat box below to meet and connect with your fellow students.


Before uploading your image make sure you visit the rules page also make sure to check the categories page to see what the categories of this month are. On this page you will be able to see what the upcoming and previous months categories. There is also a suggestion box that you can upload your own categories that we may use for our next category. If you have any questions about your submission please contact the site administrator at safaouim495@macomb.edu
